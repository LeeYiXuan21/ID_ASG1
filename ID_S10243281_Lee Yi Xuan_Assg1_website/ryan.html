Ryan Michael Dusick : Biography

Ryan Michael Dusick (born September 19, 1977) is the former and original drummer of American pop rock band Maroon 5.

The constant performing proved to be detrimental to Dusick, who wound up hurting his arm so badly from the continuous shows that he was forced to stop playing in late 2004. As he temporarily sat out to undergo tests and attempt to heal, Maroon 5 continued their heavy touring regime with musicians such as Ryland Steen and Josh Day taking Dusick's place behind the drum kit. Matt Flynn officially replaced Ryan Dusick in September 2006 .
