James Valentine : Biography

James Burgon Valentine is an American musician. He is best known as the lead guitarist of the pop rock band Maroon 5. He was also a guitarist for the indie pop band JJAMZ.

Valentine was born in Lincoln, Nebraska. He was raised as a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and has three sisters and one brother. His mother is a former beauty queen, who was a school teacher before becoming a stay-at-home mom to raise her five children. James is an Eagle Scout. He was the president of his high school Lincoln Southeast High School's student council in Lincoln, Nebraska. In addition to guitar, James is also a percussionist, playing in orchestra and jazz band in his youth.
