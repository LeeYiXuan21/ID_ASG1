Adam Levine : Biography

Adam Noah Levine was born on March 18, 1979, in Los Angeles, California, the USA to parents, Patsy Noah Levine and Fred Levine. His mother was an admissions counsellor whiles his father owns a retail chain named ‘M. Fredric’. Adam has a brother named Michael and two half-siblings, namely Sam and Liza Levine. He also has a step-sister by the name; Julia Milne Levine. As per his educational background, Adam Levine attended ‘Brentwood School’. He later joined ‘Five Towns College,’ Long Island, after he graduated from high school. He, however, dropped out after the first semester.

Jesse Royal Carmichael : Biography

Jesse Royal Carmichael (born April 2, 1979) is the keyboardist, rhythm guitarist and backing vocalist in the American pop rock band Maroon 5. He is also known for his solo project, which is called 1863 and his side project titled Circuit Jerks. Carmichael was born in Boulder, Colorado, United States. His father, Bob Carmichael, is a photographer, who also takes pictures during Maroon 5's tours. Jesse also has a younger sister, Annie. He first started playing the guitar in junior high, rather than the keyboards (for which he is known in the band Maroon 5). On March 9, 2012, Carmichael was announced to take a break from playing in Maroon 5, for an undetermined amount of time to focus more on his studies of "music and the healing arts" (spiritual healing). However, two years later (in 2014), he returned to the band in time to record for their fifth studio album, entitled V.

Ryan Michael Dusick: Biography

Ryan Michael Dusick (born September 19, 1977) is the former and original drummer of American pop rock band Maroon 5. The constant performing proved to be detrimental to Dusick, who wound up hurting his arm so badly from the continuous shows that he was forced to stop playing in late 2004. As he temporarily sat out to undergo tests and attempt to heal, Maroon 5 continued their heavy touring regime with musicians such as Ryland Steen and Josh Day taking Dusick's place behind the drum kit. Matt Flynn officially replaced Ryan Dusick in September 2006 .

Mickey Madden: Biography

Michael Allen Madden, also known as Mickey Madden (born May 13, 1979) is an American musician, who is best known for being the former bassist in the pop rock band Maroon 5. Madden was born in Austin, Texas. He began playing in junior high school, playing in garages along with friends Jesse Carmichael (guitar/vocals) and Adam Levine (vocals/guitar). The bands Pearl Jam and Nirvana were among their influences. In 1994, with the addition of Ryan Dusick (drums), the band Kara's Flowers was formed. In July of 2020 he was arrested due to an alleged incident of domestic violence, which led him to leave the band and leaves Adam as the only original member of the band to not leave the band at some of time.
